    What is a degradable plastic Bag

    A degradable plastic bag is a bag that degrades when exposed to micro-organisms, heat, and sunlight.

    What make the bag degradable

    Degradable bag are made from normal polyethylene product the same as most bags- but also contain newly invented degradable plastic additives as high as 20%

    How does it work

    Degradable additives when mixed with compatible plastic raw materials LLDPE/LDPE. Will cause the plastic to photo, thermal and chemically degraded as litter in anaerobic and aerobic facilities. These plastic will progressively degrade to lower and lower molecular weight. As a result of which they become brittle, fragmented until a point in time when they are digested by microorganisms back to the basic elements of carbon dioxide, water and the biomass.
